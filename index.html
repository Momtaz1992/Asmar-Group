About Us

Founded in 2002 by Fouad Mustafa Asmar, F.M.A is a leading import company sourcing products from China, Egypt, Syria, and Turkey. Specializing in construction materials, maritime supplies, scales, and more, F.M.A is known for understanding its clients' needs, offering competitive prices, and ensuring efficient delivery throughout Lebanon. With a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, F.M.A has become a trusted partner in the import industry, providing tailored solutions and reliable services.
